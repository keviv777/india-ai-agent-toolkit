# Example Outputs

## Example 1: Instagram Reel Script for a Personal Finance App

**User Request:** Write a short reel script explaining SIPs to a 25-year-old earning their first salary.

**Output:**
> **Hook:** Pehli salary aayi nahi ki kharche shuru? 💸 Wait karlo boss! 
> 
> **Body:** Dekho, naye phone aur sneakers toh aate rahenge, par financial freedom ek hi baar set karni hai. Agar aap apne 20s mein ho, toh FD ka zamana gaya. SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is your new best friend. 🚀 Har mahine sirf ₹1000 se start karo, aur power of compounding ka magic dekho jab aap 30 ke hoge. 
> 
> Savings account mein paisa dead hai, market mein paisa zinda hai. 📈
> 
> **Call-to-Action:** Toh socho mat, aaj hi apna pehla SIP shuru karo. Aur aisi aasan finance tips ke liye is page ko follow zaroor karna! 🙌

## Example 2: LinkedIn Post for a Startup Founder

**User Request:** Write a LinkedIn post sharing a lesson from a failed product launch.

**Output:**
> "Fail fast? Nahi, pehle seekho fast." 🧠
>
> 6 mahine lagatar code kiya, product launch hua, aur pehle din users... zero. 📉 
> 
> Humne ek aisi problem solve ki jo actual mein exist hi nahi karti thi. Market research ke naam par bas doston se aage badhkar poocha hi nahi. That was our biggest mistake. 
> 
> Us din ek bohot badi lesson seekhi: "Build what they want", na ki "Build what you think looks cool."
>
> Aaj humari nayi product line has over 10K active users. Kyunki iss baar humne pehle baat ki, aur phir code likha. 🙌 
> 
> Founders, aapka sabse bada failure aapko kya sikhakar gaya? Comments mein batao, let's learn together! 👇
